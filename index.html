Research Projects

Preparation and characterization of Thermoelectric material

  • Synthesized 6 layered thermoelectric material using solid state mechanisms in different external environments.
  • Validated material characteristics using X-ray diffraction, scanning electron microscopy (FESEM), high temperature X-ray diffraction and TG/DSC.
  • Experimentally analyzed thermoelectric properties using LSR and LFA setups.

Molecular Dynamics of charged polymer chains

  • Studied single chain polyelectrolytes and charged co-block polymer chains using molecular dynamics simulations.
  • Calculated sensitivity of physical and chemical properties of the polymer backbone (e.g. radius of gyration, end-to-end distance, ion condensation) to mechanical parameters like rigidity, dielectrics and salt concentration.
  • Gained expertise in molecular dynamics simulation using software packages like LAMMPS and VMD.
