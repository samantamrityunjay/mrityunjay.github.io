Projects

Automatic Assignment of Medical Codes

Organization: AlgoAnalytics
Date: Aug 2020 - March 2021
  • Medical codes are helpful in maintaining health records, sharing and useful in epidemiological studies. Each medical text can have more than one medical codes
  • Worked with MIMIC III dataset and used descriptive data analysis to devise a machine learning application pipeline for assigning the codes.
  • Medical text was preprocessed using various libraries such as NLTK and Spacy to extract relevant sections of the medical text from its raw form.
  • Developed a NonAI method that uses semantic relationship between medical keywords to assign the codes.
  • Implemented One vs All classifier (Logistic Regression) to improve upon the baseline Non-AI model
  • Developed a hybrid deep learning model involving CNN/RNN architecture to further improve the assignment process.
  • Further improvement was done by fine tuning the pretrained BERT-classifier

Molecular Dynamics Simulation of Polyectrolytes

Lab: Srabanti Chaudhury, IISER Pune
Date: Jun 2018 - Apr 2019
  • Studied single chain polyelectrolytes and charged co-block polymer chains using molecular dynamics simulations.
  • Calculated sensitivity of physical and chemical properties of the polymer backbone (e.g. radius of gyration, end-to-end distance, ion condensation) to mechanical parameters like rigidity, dielectrics and salt concentration.
